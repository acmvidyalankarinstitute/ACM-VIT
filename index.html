Why ACM?

Our goal is to advance the computing profession and foster a vibrant community through learning, collaboration, and innovation.

Plan

Organize and execute technical events and projects that align with the latest advancements and industry needs.

Strength

Maximize engagement by encouraging participation from a diverse range of students across different disciplines.

Uniqueness

Promote originality by collaborating with other groups to prevent overlap.

Concern for students

Promote student involvement in ACM activities to enhance their academic and professional growth.

Beyond the books

Focus on practical and cutting-edge skills that extend beyond the traditional curriculum through hands-on experiences.

Personality Development

Offer opportunities for students to hone their leadership and teamwork skills.
